    Comrade: Composable Modeling of Radio Emission

    This team has developed a foundational package in radio astronomy and the EHTJulia organization to characterize black holes and other radio emission objects via very-long baseline interferometry (VLBI) measurements.

    Package: ptiede/Comrade.jl

    See their introductory JOSS paper and companion Event Horizon Telescope (EHT) paper in The Astrophysical Journal Letters below for more. Additionally, the dozens of other radio astronomy papers citing this package can be found here, and associated JuliaCon talks can be explored here.
